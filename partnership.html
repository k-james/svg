Partnership

BECOME A PARTNER

WHO WE WORK WITH

We focus on providing optimal service to the top 1% of doctors. Partnership is at the heart of what we do, we recognize that your success has been built from your identity, brand, and expertise. We’ll bring the resources and guidance where needed, you continue doing you.

WHAT WE DO

Our partners are the backbone and heart of our company so we don’t take it lightly. We give you full authority to run your practice how you want to run it. You don’t have to worry about us micromanaging you like a DSO, we will give you the support when you want and the flexibility when you don’t. Bottom line: We are here for you!

Partners Have…

OWNERSHIP

This is your practice, not ours. We believe that mutual trust and respect is the only way to succeed in a partnership.

AUTONOMY

Do you see areas where you want to improve? Or maybe you want guidance from our experts on how to expand your practice. Either way, you have control and we will assist you.

SUPPORT

Don’t settle for the stuffy, out-of-date methods of a DSO. Get support from a practice that understands your needs and gives the power to you!

THE PARTNERSHIP PROCESS

1.

THE FIRST CHAT

Firstly, we’ll get to know eachother! We’re professionals but we genuinely want to get to know you as a person and a doctor. After that we’ll discuss your goals and growth areas.
2.

The Homework Phase

Next comes the homework phase. Let’s get that financial discussion out of the way and come up with ways that will profit your business.
3.

The Design

We will craft the design which is essentially a detailed, personalized plan on how we’ll fit together.
4.

The Decision

We’ll commit and get to work making that initial design into a reality.
5.

The Growth Phase

Gain steady growth in your practice, as well as peace of mind that your business will be taken care of. You will acquire our capital, resources, and relationships so you can grow at your own pace.

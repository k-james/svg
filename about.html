About Us

BECOME A PARTNER
Our Mission
Elevate Dental Partners has the resources that large DSOs provide, but leaves you the autonomy to make your own decisions. Because we only partner with the top 1% of doctors, we understand that your practice is uniquely yours and should be treated as such. We believe in you and strive to build a relationship rich with trust and hard work. Our mission is to show you that Elevate Dental Partners is here to support you, not the other way around.

Our Core Values

Support

Our partners benefit from autonomy. We don’t treat you like a puppet, but let you make the decisions.

Day One

For us, every day is the first. We approach every day with consistent energy and vitality as if it was our first day on the job. That means we’ll continuously seek to improve ourselves and solve problems to ensure your practice goals are met with enthusiasm and perseverance.

Ownership

We pride ourselves on our vision and our grit in making it happen. When things need to get done, we work hard, own the outcome, and hold ourselves accountable for the success.

Stewardship

Our doctors entrust us with their life’s work; our patients entrust us with their health. This isn’t just a job to us– we’ve got your back.

Teamwork

We work hard and play hard together. We believe that when you love what you do, it will motivate you to work harder. Our team is full of passionate people who put their egos aside for the benefit of the entire team.

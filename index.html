BECOME A PARTNER
Here to Help. Not to Control
We know that the administrative side of dentistry isn’t what you signed up for. You went into the dental industry to help patients thrive with beautiful smiles and excellent oral health. We applaud you for taking it all on and we understand why partnering with a DSO may feel like a last resort, but what if we told you you could have the same administrative resources as a DSO while still being in control of your practice? We started Elite Dental Partners because we believe doctors provide the best care when they’re free to be doctors.

How It Works

The Partnership Process

1.

ACCESS AREAS FOR GROWTH

Our resources, experience, and insights allow you to grow your practice in ways you never thought possible. We will first get to know you and access areas for growth, providing you with personalized insights to improve your practice.
2.

PROVIDE TAILORED SOLUTIONS

While we assess the areas needed to improve your practice and profit margins, we’ll come up with a plan fit for your needs. Whether that be growing at your current practice or starting your own empire, our tailored solutions take into account your unique needs and what you want.
3.

EXPAND YOUR BRAND
‍

With our extensive marketing services, we’ll find ways to grow your brand and drive profitability. We partner with the top marketing experts in the industry so that your one-of-a-kind voice is portrayed to your target audience.

WE’RE HERE TO HELP YOU

For Elite Dentists

HR Management

We handle the details of staffing management so that you can get the best fit for you. To get only the best of the best, we provide attractive benefits and pre-screen resumes when you need a position filled. 

  • Healthcare
  • Paid Time Off
  • 401k with a company match
Accounting

You are a doctor, not an accountant. Our experts in accounting take care of paying your bills and provide you with an organized report of your expenses. We will analyze and discuss your expenses to determine the most optimal way to keep your practice profitable.

Marketing Training

Looking to stand out? Our marketing team specializes in finding your niche and developing a brand that stays true to you while still driving growth. When you sign up for our marketing services, you will get the most cost-efficient and expert-driven marketing around.

Technology

Technology paves the way for your practice to run smoothly. With our extensive research on state-of-the-art clinical and information technology, we provide the tech resources you need to thrive.

Mentorship

Learning how to run a business is not an easy task. That’s why going to an expert who has experienced the trials and errors of running a business, and being a doctor, can set you ahead. We don’t want you to make the same mistakes as us, but we do want you to learn from our successes. With our mentorship, we will be with you every step of the way.
